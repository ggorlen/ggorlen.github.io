Class 4: CSS continued and a touch of JavaScript

In today's class, we'll wrap up CSS and begin JS. If you have any concerns about CSS, no problem-- we'll have plenty of time in the coming weeks to make it understandable for everyone. As always, consult this week's lesson and ask questions of your fellow students or the instructor.


Today's topics:

  1. Recap Tuesday's lesson on CSS and ask/answer questions.
  2. Do a quick show and tell of a couple students' pages.
  3. Take 30 minutes to work on your site with a buddy and accomplish the following tasks:
    • Clean up, organize, and ask questions about your code.
    • Make a second page other than index.html called about.html.
    • Link the pages together with <a> tags, so that users can seamlessly navigate between the two pages.
    • Research new CSS properties to use to style your page.
    • View source on this page for tips!
  4. Discuss Bootstrap
  5. Discuss JavaScript and introduce variables and the console.log() function.
  6. Before leaving:
    1. Shut down your computers and plug them in to a power source on the cart.
    2. Put your chair up.
    3. Work on your website over the weekend if you have time! You can add more pages, content, and style/layout components. The only way to learn HTML and CSS is to practice by making a ton of pages!
